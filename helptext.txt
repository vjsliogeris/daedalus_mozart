Commands:

`#!help` for this menu.

`#!p $songname$` For playing songs. The song will be added to be next in the queue.
e.g.
`#!p Outkast Roses`
`#!p https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wpV-gGA4PSk`

`#!shuffle` to shuffle the playlist randomly.

`#!q` For the next 10 songs in the queue + the current song.

`#!q $n$` for the next $n$ songs in the queue (i.e. `#!q 20` for the next 20 songs)

`#!skip` to skip.
